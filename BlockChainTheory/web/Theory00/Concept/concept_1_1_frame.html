Block Chain Concepts

Wallets

To spend your funds you need the digital keys to access your public bitcoin addresses and sign transactions. These private keys are stored in the socalled wallets.

There are five main types of wallet: desktop, mobile, web, paper and hardware.


  • Desktop wallets Client running on Windows, MAC OSX and Linux that allows the user to send and receive digital currency, and to store his private keys.
  • Mobile wallets Apps for smartphones to pay in physical stores.
  • Online wallets A way to store private keys online so that keys access does not depend on a specific device.
  • Hardware wallets Dedicated devices to store private keys.
