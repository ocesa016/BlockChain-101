Smart Contracts

A word of caution

DANGER !!!

One of the strenght of the smart contract the program itself is recorded on the blockchain is also one of the big weakness.

The program becomes immutable, hence it is not possible to update it in case of Bugs.

Patterns exists to overcome the problem, but those patterns also allow the contract owner to change the contract terms unilaterally.

Rule of Tumb: Be carefull when dealing with smart contracts in permiossionless block chains. Use extra cautions where your counterpart is the owner / creator of the contract.
