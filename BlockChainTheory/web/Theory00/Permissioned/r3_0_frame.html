History

LR3 (R3CEV LLC) is a blockchain technology company. It leads a consortium of 45 financial companies in research and development of blockchain usage in the financial system. It is headquartered in New York City. It was founded circa 2014

January, 2016. An 11-bank trial which used Ethereum hosted on Microsoft Azure.

On March 3, 2016, R3 announced that it had completed a trial involving 40 banks held in the last two weeks of February, testing the use of blockchain solutions offered by Eris Industries, IBM, Intel and Chain to facilitate the trading of debt instruments.
