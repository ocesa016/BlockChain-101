Block Chain Adoptions

The New Land Of Opportunity

A number of company and startup are exploring and exprimenting on the possibility that the block chain technology offers to different businnes segments.


  • WAVE connects all members of the supply chain to a decentralized network and allows them a direct exchange of documents. WAVE's application manages ownership of documents on the blockchain eliminating disputes, forgeries and unnecessary risks.
    An article is available here
  • OpenBazar new way to buy and sell goods and services online. By running a program on your computer, you can connect directly to other users in the OpenBazaar network and trade with them. The network isn't controlled by a company or organisation. OpenBazaar is a decentralised peer-to-peer network, which means there are no listing fees and the marketplace is censorship-resistant. Goods and services are bought and sold on OpenBazaar using Bitcoin, a digital cryptocurrency that is decentralised and censorship-resistant. Transaction fees on the Bitcoin network are very cheap.
    Winner of 2016 Bitcoin Award
  • Everleger We are a fraud detection system, overlaying big data from closed sources like insurers and law enforcement. Technology can be extended to track any asset that carries a Unique Identifier which is difficult to destroy or replicate. (Diamonds, art..)
    Smart Contracts Based. Heris blockchain + bitcon, will then move to ethereum.
